This letter provides an update on Atlas Capital Partners Fund IV portfolio activity and

FUND IV PORTFOLIO SUMMARY

  Portfolio companies (active):         8

  Weighted average gross IRR (unrealized): 23.4%
  Q1 2024 exit:                         Brightpath Logistics — 2.8x MOIC, 31% IRR

We are pleased with the Brightpath realization, which came in ahead of the original 5-year
hold plan. The exit reflected strong demand from strategic acquirers in the logistics
software space.

DEAL PIPELINE UPDATE

The deal team has been active in the B2B fintech infrastructure segment, consistent with
our Fund IV sector thesis. We have one transaction in advanced diligence — internally
designated Project Clearwater — which we expect to bring before the Investment Committee
this month. We are not disclosing the target at this stage but anticipate announcing a
new platform investment in Q2 or Q3 2024, subject to final negotiations.

Additional pipeline includes two payments technology targets at earlier diligence stages
(Projects Ridgeline and Shoreline) and one enterprise data platform target being evaluated
for a co-investment opportunity alongside a strategic partner.

MARKET OBSERVATIONS

The software M&A environment remains favorable for disciplined buyers. Valuations in the
fintech infrastructure segment have moderated significantly from their 2021 peak — quality
assets are trading at 8–12x ARR today, versus 18–25x at the prior market peak. This creates
meaningful return potential for long-duration, operationally focused ownership.

We will present a full portfolio review and Q1 2024 valuations at the LP Advisory Board
meeting scheduled for May 2024. Please reach out to investor relations with any questions.
