ATLAS CAPITAL PARTNERS — INTERNAL ANALYSIS
PROJECT CLEARWATER: CUSTOMER CONCENTRATION RISK ASSESSMENT
April 8, 2024 | CONFIDENTIAL — VP AND ABOVE

PREPARED BY: Diana Park, VP

OVERVIEW

This memo summarizes the findings of our customer concentration analysis for Meridian
Technologies, based on the customer ARR schedule provided by management on April 3, 2024.

CONCENTRATION FINDINGS (AS OF MARCH 31, 2024, ESTIMATED)

  Customer 1 (Regional Bank A):       $9.4M ARR     24.7% of total
  Customer 2 (Regional Bank B):       $7.2M ARR     18.9% of total
  Customer 3 (Insurance Co. C):       $5.7M ARR     15.0% of total
  Top 3 Subtotal:                     $22.3M ARR    58.5% of total
  Remaining 44 customers:             $15.8M ARR    41.5% of total
  Total ARR:                          $38.1M

Customer identities are masked per management's NDA request. Legal names available
under full data room access.

KEY OBSERVATIONS

1. CONCENTRATION INCREASED MATERIALLY

The Q3 2023 Summit Financial Research note cited ~45% ARR concentration in the top two
customers. Our analysis shows top-three concentration is now 58.5%, a meaningful increase.
This reflects the H2 2023 new logo slowdown rather than outsized growth from top clients.

2. CUSTOMER 3 RENEWAL RISK

Customer 3 (Insurance Co. C) has a contract renewal due in August 2024. This date falls
within the anticipated deal announcement window and represents a pre-close risk item.
If Customer 3 exits, total ARR would drop to approximately $32.4M, putting significant
pressure on the acquisition thesis at any entry multiple above 7x ARR.

3. IMPACT ON FINANCIAL MODEL

Financial Model v1.0 assumed 35% ARR growth without a concentration stress test.
Model v2.0 (to be released April 10) will incorporate revised growth assumptions and a
customer-loss scenario in the bear case.

RECOMMENDATION

Require audited customer contract terms and renewal schedules for the top five customers as
a Phase 2 diligence condition. Consider inserting a revenue concentration covenant in the
deal terms (e.g., no single customer >25% of ARR within 24 months post-close).
