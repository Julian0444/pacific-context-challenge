TECH PRESS ARTICLE — Meridian rumored $500M valuation based on public market chatter, not internal diligence or Atlas materials.
March 28, 2024 | PUBLIC NEWS SOURCE

Fintech Market Wire has learned from two people familiar with recent financing conversations that Meridian Technologies may be seeking strategic alternatives at a rumored $500M valuation. The figure appears to be based on public SaaS multiples, Meridian's reported $38.1M FY2023 ARR, and prior growth commentary from third-party research notes.

The rumored valuation conflicts with more conservative internal buyer work that values Meridian closer to the mid-$300M range after factoring in slower FY2024 ARR growth, customer concentration, and revised new-logo assumptions. Atlas Capital declined to comment. Meridian also declined to comment on any sale process.

Market observers note that regional-bank infrastructure assets remain attractive despite a cooler software M&A environment. Meridian's ClearFlow platform has strong retention metrics and a credible product niche, but buyers are said to be focused on the company's top-customer exposure and the timing of a major enterprise renewal in August 2024.

This article is based entirely on public filings, market checks, and investor conversations. It does not reflect confidential diligence, board materials, lender workpapers, or any non-public financial model.
