SECTOR THESIS

The market for B2B financial infrastructure software continues to consolidate. We see a two-to-
three year window where embedded payments, reconciliation automation, and compliance tooling will
attract significant PE and strategic buyer interest, driven by:

  (1) Regional banks seeking to modernize back-office operations without building in-house
  (2) Increasing regulatory complexity favoring specialized software providers
  (3) Attractive SaaS metrics in the segment (NRR >110%, gross margins >65%)

TARGET PROFILE

Our preferred acquisition profile within this sector:

  ARR:             $30–$60M
  ARR growth:      >20% year-over-year
  NRR:             >110%
  Gross margin:    >65%
  Customer base:   Enterprise, multi-year contracts
  Debt:            Minimal, positive unit economics

IDENTIFIED TARGETS

  1. Meridian Technologies — Austin, TX. ARR ~$38M, NRR 118%, gross margin 71%.
     ClearFlow platform for payment reconciliation. Currently Series C. Customer
     concentration is a noted risk requiring diligence.

  2. Trident Payments — Chicago, IL. ARR ~$21M. Earlier stage, growing at 40% YoY.
     Focused on credit union segment. May be too early for our fund thesis.

  3. VaultSync — New York, NY. ARR ~$55M. More mature, slower growth (~15% YoY).
     Potential buyout at a lower multiple; less value-creation upside.

RECOMMENDED FOCUS

Meridian Technologies is the primary diligence target. The NRR and gross margin profile are
best-in-class within our identified universe. Customer concentration is a key risk to underwrite,
but at current revenue scale it is addressable through an active sales expansion program
post-acquisition.

Deal team to initiate outreach to Meridian management in Q1 2024.
