EXCERPT: BUSINESS OVERVIEW AND SELECTED FINANCIAL DATA

BUSINESS OVERVIEW

Meridian Technologies, Inc. is a B2B fintech platform headquartered in Austin, Texas. The Company
provides embedded payment infrastructure and real-time reconciliation software to mid-market and
enterprise financial institutions across the United States. Meridian's flagship product, ClearFlow,
enables banks and financial institutions to automate payment exception handling and back-office
reconciliation at scale.

As of December 31, 2023, Meridian served 47 enterprise clients, including six of the top 20 U.S.
regional banks. Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) grew from $28.4 million in fiscal year 2022 to
$38.1 million in fiscal year 2023, representing 34.2% year-over-year growth.

RISK FACTORS (SUMMARY)

Management identifies the following as primary business risks:

  (i)  Customer revenue concentration — the Company's three largest customers represent
       approximately 58% of ARR as of December 31, 2023.
  (ii) Dependence on continued adoption of embedded finance solutions by regional banking
       institutions, which may be affected by regulatory changes or macroeconomic conditions.
  (iii) Competitive pressure from established payments infrastructure providers, including
       large technology companies entering the reconciliation automation market.
