RESEARCH NOTE UPDATE — MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES
Summit Financial Research | January 15, 2024

MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES: REVISING ESTIMATES DOWNWARD; RATING MAINTAINED AT BUY

We are revising our September 2023 financial projections for Meridian Technologies following
the release of Q3 2023 earnings data and updated channel checks.

REVISION SUMMARY

Our FY2024 ARR growth estimate is revised downward from 35% to 22%, reflecting:

  — Slower-than-expected enterprise contract closings: one of two anticipated H1 2024
    top-20 bank signings has been pushed to H2 2024 per management commentary
  — Customer concentration risk is higher than our September 2023 assessment. Updated
    channel checks indicate Meridian's top three customers now represent approximately
    58–62% of ARR, up from ~45% cited in our initiation note
  — Delayed FX module launch (rescheduled from Q4 2023 to Q2 2024)

UPDATED FINANCIAL PROJECTIONS

  FY2024 ARR estimate (revised):   $46.5 million  (prior: $51.4 million)
  Gross margin assumption:          71%  (unchanged)
  Revised EV range:                 $300–$360 million

MAINTAINED BUY RATIONALE

Despite the downward revision, we maintain our BUY rating. Meridian's NRR of 118% (per
preliminary FY2023 data) remains a key differentiator. The ClearFlow platform's stickiness
within regional bank workflows is difficult to displace, and we view the contract delay as
a timing issue rather than structural demand erosion.

The customer concentration increase warrants monitoring. We expect Meridian to actively
diversify its customer base through 2024 as the sales team scales.

Rating: BUY | Revised EV Target: $340M | Date: January 15, 2024
(Replaces September 20, 2023 research note)
