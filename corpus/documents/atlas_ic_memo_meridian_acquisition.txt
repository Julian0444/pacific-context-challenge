ATLAS CAPITAL PARTNERS
INVESTMENT COMMITTEE MEMORANDUM
PROJECT CLEARWATER: ACQUISITION OF MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES, INC.
April 18, 2024 | STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL — PARTNER ACCESS ONLY

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Atlas Capital Partners Fund IV recommends proceeding with the acquisition of Meridian
Technologies, Inc. at an enterprise value of $340 million, subject to final legal and
financial confirmations. This memorandum presents the full investment rationale, risk
summary, and proposed deal terms for Investment Committee approval.

INVESTMENT RATIONALE

1. DEFENSIBLE MARKET POSITION
   Meridian's ClearFlow platform holds a strong position in payment reconciliation for U.S.
   regional banks. Average customer tenure of 4.2 years among the top-10 accounts and a 118%
   NRR confirm the platform's stickiness. Switching cost is high; implementation cycles of 4–6
   months create structural retention.

2. FINANCIAL PROFILE
   FY2023 ARR of $38.1M, 71% gross margins, zero debt. While FY2024 growth has been revised
   from initial 35% estimates to 22% (reflecting customer concentration dynamics and delayed
   contract signings), the base business remains highly cash-generative at scale.

3. VALUE CREATION LEVERS
   (a) Enterprise sales expansion to accelerate new logo growth and reduce top-3 concentration
       below 45% within 24 months
   (b) International expansion into UK and EU embedded finance markets (Year 3+)
   (c) Adjacent product extension: treasury management automation, compliance tooling

RISKS AND MITIGATIONS

  Customer Concentration:     Top 3 = 58% of ARR.
                               Mitigation: Negotiate 3–5 year contract extensions pre-close.
  CTO Departure:              Mid-refactor on reconciliation engine.
                               Mitigation: Retention packages for VP Engineering + top 5 engineers.
  August 2024 Renewal Risk:   Customer 3 renewal coincides with deal announcement.
                               Mitigation: Negotiate renewal or assignment clause before signing.
  Net Cash Adjustment:         $22.6M reported cash includes $4.1M deferred revenue.
                               Modeled as $18.5M adjusted net cash.

PROPOSED DEAL TERMS

  Enterprise Value:            $340 million
  Implied ARR Multiple:        8.9x
  Equity Check (Fund IV):      $240 million
  Debt Financing:              $100 million (3.2x leverage)
  Management Rollover:         ~8% of equity
  Target Signing:              May 2024
  Target Close:                Q3 2024

RECOMMENDATION: APPROVE for final negotiation and signing.
