MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES CLOSES $45 MILLION SERIES C FINANCING

Austin, TX — June 15, 2023 — Meridian Technologies, Inc., a leading provider of embedded payment
infrastructure for financial institutions, today announced the closing of a $45 million Series C
financing round. The round was led by Parkview Growth Equity, with participation from existing
investors Bridgewater Ventures and Cascade Capital.

Meridian will use the proceeds to accelerate product development, expand its enterprise sales team,
and pursue strategic partnerships within the U.S. regional banking sector.

"We built ClearFlow to solve a real infrastructure problem for banks trying to modernize without
ripping out legacy systems," said CEO James Hartley. "This funding gives us the runway to double
down on what's working and expand into adjacent workflows."

COMPANY HIGHLIGHTS AT TIME OF CLOSING:

  — ARR:               $27.1 million (as of May 2023)
  — Net Revenue Retention:  114%
  — Enterprise Clients:     39 financial institutions
  — Employees:          168 full-time

Parkview Growth Equity Partner Sandra Yuen added: "Meridian is one of the most capital-efficient
fintech infrastructure companies we have evaluated. The ClearFlow platform has become deeply
embedded in its clients' operations, and we believe the opportunity in regional bank modernization
is still in the early innings."

The Series C brings Meridian's total funding to $72 million since founding in 2018.

ABOUT MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES
Meridian Technologies, Inc. provides embedded payment infrastructure and reconciliation automation
software to financial institutions. The Company is headquartered in Austin, Texas.
