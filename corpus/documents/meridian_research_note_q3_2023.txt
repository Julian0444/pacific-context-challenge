RESEARCH NOTE — MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES
COVERAGE INITIATION
Summit Financial Research | September 20, 2023

MERIDIAN TECHNOLOGIES: INITIATING COVERAGE WITH BUY

We initiate coverage of Meridian Technologies with a BUY rating and a base-case enterprise
value target of $480 million.

INVESTMENT THESIS

Meridian is positioned to capture significant share in the $14 billion back-office reconciliation
and embedded payments market. Its ClearFlow platform has demonstrated strong product-market fit
with regional banks, a segment underserved by legacy processors. Net Revenue Retention of 116%
as of Q2 2023 supports a durable land-and-expand revenue model.

FINANCIAL PROJECTIONS

We project 35% ARR growth in fiscal year 2024, driven by:

  — Two new top-20 bank contract signings expected in H1 2024
  — Expansion revenue from existing clients as reconciliation volumes grow
  — Planned launch of FX and multi-currency modules in Q4 2023

  FY2024 ARR estimate:     $51.4 million
  Valuation basis:          12.5x ARR multiple → EV $642 million
  Risk-adjusted base case:  $480 million enterprise value

RISKS

  — Revenue concentration: approximately 45% of ARR from top two customers
  — Execution risk on new product launches
  — Dependency on regional bank vertical health

We view near-term concentration risk as manageable given Meridian's strong NRR and the
company's growing client diversification strategy. New logo pipeline for H2 2023 appears
robust based on our channel checks with industry contacts.

Rating: BUY | EV Target: $480M | Date: September 20, 2023
